How to Request Account Deletion

Since Smart Sense Field Reports is an internal business application for authorized employees, account deletion is handled by the system administrator.

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

✉️ ahmedtarek.apps@gmail.com
Please include your username, employee name, and employee code in your email so we can process your request quickly.
📋

Deletion Process

  1. Send a deletion request email to ahmedtarek.apps@gmail.com with your account details.

  2. The administrator will verify your identity and confirm the request within 3 business days.

  3. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

🗂️

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (username, password)
  • Your employee profile (name, code, role)
  • All field reports you have submitted
  • All notifications associated with your account
  • Vacation records
  • Activity audit logs
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
⏱️

Data Retention After Request

After your deletion request is confirmed, your data will be removed within 7 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated and you will not be able to log in.

If you change your mind, you may cancel your request by contacting us before the deletion is processed.
