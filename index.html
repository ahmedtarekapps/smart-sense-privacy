Privacy Policy

Smart Sense Field Reports Application

📅 Last updated: April 17, 2026
📋

Overview

Smart Sense Field Reports ("the App") is an internal business application developed for field supervisors, training teams, and retail merchandisers to submit daily activity reports.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This App is intended for authorized employees only. It is not available to the general public.
📊

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username and encrypted password (stored on our secure server)
  • Employee name and employee code
  • Role / team assignment (Supervisor, Training, or RM)

Report Data

  • Daily field visit reports including branch visits, sales data, and activity notes
  • Report submission date and time
  • Branch / store information associated with each report

Device & Technical Data

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) stored locally on your device for session management
  • Biometric authentication preference (enabled/disabled flag only — no biometric data is stored)
⚠️ We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any personal data beyond what is listed above.
⚙️

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the App
  • To store and display your submitted field reports
  • To allow management to review and respond to reports
  • To send in-app notifications about report status updates
  • To maintain security audit logs for account activity

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

🛡️

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on a secure, private server protected by HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (PBKDF2-SHA256)
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT tokens (1-hour expiry) with refresh tokens
  • The server is protected by Cloudflare WAF and firewall rules
  • Only authorized administrators can access the backend database
All communication between the App and our server is encrypted using TLS 1.2/1.3.
👆

Biometric Authentication

The App offers optional fingerprint / face ID login for convenience.

  • Biometric data (fingerprints, face data) is never collected, stored, or transmitted by the App
  • Biometric verification is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android Biometric API)
  • We only store a simple flag (enabled/disabled) in your device's local storage
🗂️

Data Retention

Report data is retained for as long as required for business operations. When an employee account is deleted by an administrator, all associated reports and data are permanently removed from our servers.

Local data on your device (authentication tokens, preferences) is cleared when you log out or uninstall the App.

✅

Your Rights

As an authorized user of this App, you have the right to:

  • Access the reports you have submitted
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting your administrator
  • Disable biometric login at any time from the login screen
📱

App Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to communicate with our server to submit and retrieve reports
  • USE_BIOMETRIC — Required for optional fingerprint/face ID login
  • USE_FINGERPRINT — Legacy support for fingerprint on older Android versions

No other permissions are requested.
